# Introduction
I am a Data Visualization student at the University of Washington Bothell with a focus on turning data into clear, actionable insights that support real-world decision-making.

My work centers on the full analytics pipeline, from data cleaning and transformation to analysis and communication. I have experience working with large datasets using Python, R, SQL, and Tableau to identify patterns in customer behavior, product performance, and service trends. Across projects, I focus on making data understandable and useful rather than simply descriptive.

Recently, I built DecisionCanvas, an AI-powered analytics system that converts raw business data into structured insights, visual dashboards, and decision-oriented recommendations. This reflects my broader interest in how data systems can move beyond reporting and play a more direct role in strategy and operations.

I am particularly interested in roles in data analytics, business intelligence, and product analytics, where data is used to guide decisions, improve processes, and uncover opportunities.
